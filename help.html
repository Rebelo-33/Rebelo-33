How to use the site:

  • Create a New List: Click "Create a New List" on the home page. Add unique names and save it with a 4-digit PIN.
  • Save Your List: After entering names, click "Save List". A link will be generated to share with participants.
  • Access My Lists: Use the 4-digit PIN to retrieve and view any list you've created.
  • Draw a Name: Participants can draw a name from the shared link. Each name can only be drawn once.
  • View Drawn Name: If you revisit the draw page, your drawn name will be shown again without redrawing.
  • Copy or Send: You can copy your drawn name or send it to your email.
