Help & Instructions

  • Create a New List: Use the “Create a New List” button to add participants, give a name to your list, and set a 4-digit PIN to protect your list.
  • Save List: Click “Save List” to generate a shareable link with your PIN for participants to access the draw.
  • Draw Page: Participants use the PIN on the “Go to Draw Page” to draw a unique name. They can revisit to see the list or the name drawn.
  • My Lists: Organizers can view their previously created lists and manage them.
  • Help: This page contains guidance on using the app.
