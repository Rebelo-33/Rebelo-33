Help - Secret Gift Exchange

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I participate and organise?
Yes! As the organiser, you can also participate in the draw just like any other participant. This keeps the fun inclusive and allows you to be part of the exchange too.

Can I draw twice?
No. Each participant can only draw one name. To ensure fairness, your result is stored in your browser (local storage), preventing multiple draws from the same device.

I forgot my PIN or Secret Code. What should I do?
For security reasons, PINs and secret codes are not recoverable. This is to protect the privacy of all lists. If you are a participant, ask the organiser to resend the access link. If you are the organiser, you may need to create a new list with a new PIN and secret code.
